Welcome to Pikture!

Pikture is a media distribution website that makes the connections between talented independent artists and customers who seeks for art possible and simple. With the latest technology in web development that our site is using, we developed this user-friendly interface for each of our users, whether they're an artist or a customer, ensuring them for a smooth and satisfying experience.
