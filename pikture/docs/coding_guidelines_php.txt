GUIDELINES FOR PROGRAMMING PIKTURE IN PHP
[Reference to general guidelines here: http://www.php-fig.org/psr/psr-2/]

-All indents must be 4 spaces (not tabs)
-Variable names must use snake-case (example: my_variable)
-Class names must be upper-camel-case (example: MyClass)
-function names must be came-case (example: myFunction)
-Comments should be at the block level, not the line level
